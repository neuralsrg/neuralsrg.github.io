I received bachelor's and master's degrees in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at [Moscow State University](https://www.topuniversities.com/universities/lomonosov-moscow-state-university), where I studied at the Faculty of Computational Mathematics and Cybernetics. My bachelor's thesis explored the applications of discrete mathematics and combinatorics in machine learning. During my master's studies, I worked with recommender systems as a senior R&D engineer at Yandex. A list of my publications can be found in the "Publications" section.

Currently, I am planning to continue academic research and pursue a PhD. **My research interests** include (but are not limited to) information retrieval, reinforcement learning, debiasing, conversational and explainable recommender systems, generative models and scaling laws, as well as the general underlying mathematics of deep learning (e.g., Bayesian methods).

Find more details about me in the "CV" section.
