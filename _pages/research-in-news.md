* [Argus]({{ site.baseurl }}/publication/2025-07-21-argus) received broad attention after its launch. It was featured in news outlets including [ict.moscow](https://ict.moscow/news/iandeks-vnedriaet-v-rekomendatelnye-sistemy-svoikh-servisov-reshenie-argus-na-baze-generativnogo-ii/), [yagla.ru](https://yagla.ru/blog/kontekstnaya-reklama/yandeks-predstavil-krupneyshee-obnovlenie-rekomendatelnyh-sistem-ii-budet-zapominat-deystviya-i-interesy-polzovateley-za-god--26/), [mltimes.ai](https://mltimes.ai/yandeks-vnedryaet-novuyu-rekomendatelnuyu-ii-sistemu-argus/), [setka.ru](https://setka.ru/posts/01971ff6-c0ec-4ef4-b81e-a90e7e6a20ba), [toolarium.ru](https://toolarium.ru/yandex-argus-rekomendacii/), [m.seonews.ru](https://m.seonews.ru/events/yandeks-zapuskaet-novye-rekomendatelnye-sistemy-na-osnove-generativnykh-modeley/), [rozetked.me](https://rozetked.me/news/39891-yandeks-razrabotal-i-vnedril-novoe-pokolenie-rekomendaciy-na-baze-generativnyh-modeley), and [pikabu.ru](https://pikabu.ru/story/yandeks_apnul_rekomendatsii_s_pomoshchyu_generativnyikh_ii_12864778), covered on [habr.com](https://habr.com/ru/news/920204/), and discussed on [x.com](https://x.com/yandex/status/1935977821622481330) and [habr.com](https://habr.com/ru/companies/yandex/articles/919058/). It was later adapted by colleagues to many other Yandex products. Its deployment in Yandex Ads also drew extensive discussion, including [timeweb.com](https://timeweb.com/ru/community/articles/yandeks-vnedril-rekomendatelnuyu-tehnologiyu-argus-v-reklamnye-sistemy), [habr.com](https://habr.com/ru/news/1024284/), [adindex.ru](https://adindex.ru/news/digital/2026/04/14/344192.phtml), [habr.com](https://habr.com/ru/companies/click/news/1024506/), [dyvannyi-it.ru](https://dyvannyi-it.ru/yandex-argus/), [marketplacegu.ru](https://marketplacegu.ru/contentzavod/blog/view/yandeks-uvelichil-effektivnost-reklamy-na-38-s), and [sostav.ru](https://www.sostav.ru/publication/yandeks-vnedril-v-reklamnuyu-sistemu-rekomendatelnuyu-tekhnologiyu-argus-83114.html). [Sumit Kumar](https://x.com/_reachsumit?s=21), author of a popular information retrieval blog, wrote about Argus on [x.com](https://x.com/_reachsumit/status/1947864588541935620?s=20) and listed it in [Top Information Retrieval Papers of the Week](https://open.substack.com/pub/recsys/p/extending-graphrag-to-millions-of?utm_campaign=post-expanded-share&utm_medium=web).
